Company Overview

We are a waste-to-wealth startup focused on transforming recycled materials—including agro-waste, recycled PET, and other waste streams—into high-value products such as 3D printing filaments, packaging solutions, and functional components.

🎯 Mission

Our Mission

At the core of our innovation is an indigenously developed, IoT-enabled automated extruder designed for precision and scalability. Our broader mission is to enable sustainable, circular manufacturing across sectors.

The founding team comprises PhD holders in manufacturing with deep expertise in materials processing, smart automation, and sustainable product development. Our team also includes dedicated research scholars working on cutting-edge projects in energy storage devices and innovative 3D printing materials. By combining advanced engineering with environmental responsibility, we aim to redefine waste as a valuable resource for future-ready applications.
